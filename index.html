Onion Storage

For optimal freshness, store onions in a cool, dry area with temperatures between 45-55°F (7-13°C). Use breathable containers like mesh bags or slotted bins to ensure proper airflow and prevent moisture buildup. Keep onions away from direct sunlight to avoid sprouting. Regularly inspect for any signs of spoilage and remove affected onions immediately. Store onions separately from potatoes and other produce to extend their shelf life and maintain quality.
